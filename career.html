Career With Us

UKIBS is well experienced in the beauty industry. In the meantime, we’ve grown extensively by training over 3500 students and operating in 5 different countries. At UKIBS, we are known as one of the fastest-growing beauty training academies. We offer certified beauty courses, train, and groom students to become professionals in the beauty and wellness industry. Not just training students alone, UKIBS also hire skilled professionals who will impact the latest techniques needed for work experience to our students.The academy has grown and, in turn, led to the creation of massive employment opportunities for those who are willing to teach, model, franchise, and train students in the beauty and wellness industry across the country. Also, we do put out adverts on vacancies and select the best fit for our academy. To apply for any vacancy position, kindly send your resume to info@uk-international.com.State the position you’re applying for on the subject of the mail.
