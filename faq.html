  • What happens at the end of the course?

    • We don’t wait for the course to end before we start planning your career. All plans have begun from day one you enrolled. We have the know-how of network building and contacts. Whatever the career path you choose e. g working as a makeup artist or freelancer, we prepare you for it. Also, we encourage our students to be a part of our events to gain exposure and add credits to their names. We make sure they succeed by pushing them towards success.

    • We teach in English. And Hindi both . We assume that makeup is more practical and has no language. With demonstrations, you should be able to follow instructions. If you’re still not comfortable with that, we can assist you by teaching you in any language. Trust us, we’ve got you covered.

  • How does pursuing a course from UKIBS helps in shaping my career?

    • All the certified courses we offer in UKIBS are recognized and accepted by educational institutes and workplaces worldwide in the respective area of beauty and wellness you choose to go. Such certification shows that you have completed the required training and are equipped with the right skillset to perform your job role.

    • As we know, the demand for professionals in the beauty and wellness industry is on the highside. Therefore, there is no limit to the available jobs in this industry. Jobs can range from cosmetologist, makeup artist, hairstylist, etc.

    • 100% job placement assistance is guaranteed. On completing our course, outstanding students with “A” certification can get employed at UKIBS.

    • Good hygiene and work etiquette
    • Patience with clients
    • Good listening abilities
    • Politeness
    • Customer satisfaction
